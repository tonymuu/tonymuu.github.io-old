Introduction

Koalie is a mobile app that enables people to capture life experiences with others in a new and immersive way. Friends or strangers can collaborate by first connecting on Koalie and then taking video and photos of any shared life experience or event.

Whether youâ€™re going to a concert, taking a weekend road trip, going on vacation, or at a business retreat, Koalie will be there to help you capture it all. Once your event is over, Koalie will take the combined media from each person and produce an interactive recap video for everyone to view, share and download, immediately after the event ends.

Technologies

I used Swift and UIKit to develop the app's front end, while I used Node.js, Express, and MongoDB as the app's backend. The app authenticates users with Facebook SDK and also saves data remotely to Amazon S3. It allows users to take pictures and videos and share the medias with other users, interact them, and finally it is able to generate a short clip of video from an event a group of users attend and contribute to.
