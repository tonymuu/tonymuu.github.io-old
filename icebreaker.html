IceBreaker by Tony Mu @HackMizzou2016

Introduction

I developed IceBreaker app and its backend server during HackMizzou2016, with the help of two Android Developers and a UI designer. IceBreaker is a iOS app that helps you start engaging conversation with people you would absolutely love to meet.

Make Friends - And maybe one friend becomes someone more than a friend. IceBreaker helps you engage in natural and fun conversations. Showing you three interests a poetential friend has, you can start conversations in a most comfortable way.

Grasp the Moment - 90% of the people you saw today are 100% of the people you will never see again. IceBreaker encourages you to socialize and have fun with others right here, right now. With IceBreaker, you become more confident and more attractive. You make potential friends real friends.

People Nearby - No more fake people like other social app. No more messaging a stranger behind a screen. Know who you are talking to, and start talking to them! IceBreaker only shows real people around you who are also using IceBreaker. Start breaking ice, start making friends!

Technologies

I used Swift and UIKit to develop the app's front end, while I used Node.js, Express, and MongoDB as the app's backend.
